# 🛍 E-Commerce Recommendation System – Machine Learning Project  

🚀 **Personalized Product Recommendations using ML**  

## **🔹 Overview**  
This project builds an **ML-powered recommendation system** that suggests products to users based on their past behavior and item preferences.  
It leverages **Collaborative Filtering, Content-Based Filtering, and Hybrid models** to optimize recommendations and improve customer engagement.  

## **🔹 Key Features & Steps**
1️⃣ **Data Collection & Preprocessing**  
- Cleaned and transformed user-item interaction data.  
- Performed **feature engineering** to enhance recommendation accuracy.  

2️⃣ **Building Recommendation Models**  
✔ **Collaborative Filtering** – Uses **user-user & item-item similarity** to recommend products.  
✔ **Content-Based Filtering** – Matches **product features** with user interests.  
✔ **Hybrid Model** – Combines collaborative & content-based filtering for improved results.  

3️⃣ **Evaluation & Performance Metrics**  
✔ **Root Mean Square Error (RMSE)** to measure model accuracy.  
✔ **Precision-Recall & Hit Rate** to assess recommendation effectiveness.  

4️⃣ **Data Visualization with Power BI**  
✔ Built **interactive dashboards** to analyze user behavior and recommendation performance.  
✔ Visualized trends in product recommendations, purchase patterns, and interactions.  

## **🔹 Technologies Used**
- **Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn, Surprise Library)**  
- **Power BI for Visualizations**  
- **Collaborative & Content-Based Filtering Models**  
- **Evaluation Metrics (RMSE, Precision, Recall)**  

## **📌 Why This Project Matters**
This recommendation system **enhances user experience in e-commerce** by **personalizing product suggestions**, improving customer engagement, and boosting sales conversions.
